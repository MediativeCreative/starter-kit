Top tips
  • For all hair types, spray Batiste™ all over clean hair for extra body and grip when styling
  • For colour treated brown hair, use Batiste™ Divine Dark or Beautiful Brunette. It can help prevent fading and is a great way to extend the time between home colouring treatments or your next salon visit.
  • For sky high volume, use Batiste™ XXL or Heavenly volume. Tip hair upside down and spray into roots.
